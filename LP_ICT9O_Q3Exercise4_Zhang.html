Tagaytay


"Tagaytay is a popular holiday town south of Manila on the Philippine island Luzon. Known for its mild climate, it sits on a ridge above Taal Volcano Island, an active volcano surrounded by Taal Lake. Overlooking the area, People’s Park in the Sky occupies the grounds of a never-finished presidential mansion. Picnic Grove is a recreation area with trails and a zip line. ― Google

We visited Tagaytay a few weeks ago, and the scenery of the place was amazing. Looking at Taal Volcano was a unique experience—as I learned that Taal isn't just one volcano. In fact, it's a whole island, and the ground I was standing on was part of the volcano.
